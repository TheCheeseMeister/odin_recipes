Pizza

Here's a recipe on how to go about making a very epic pizza for the whole family.

Before we get started, here's what you're gonna need:

  • Yeast
  • Sugar
  • Bread Flour
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt

Now that we got that out of the way, here's the steps to making this fine dish:

  1. Preheat oven to 450 degrees F.
  2. Place warm water in a bowl. Add yeast and sugar. Mix and let stand until its creamy; around 10 mins.
  3. Add flour/oil/salt to the yeast mixture; beat until smooth by hand or with a standard mixer and dough hook.
  4. Let rest for 5 mins.
